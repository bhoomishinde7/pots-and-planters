Plant Care Guide

Learn how to care for your plants and keep them healthy with our easy-to-follow tips!

Watering

Water your plants regularly, but avoid overwatering. Most plants need water when the topsoil is dry.

Sunlight

Ensure your plants get enough light. Some plants thrive in full sun, while others prefer shade.

Soil & Fertilizer

Use nutrient-rich soil and feed your plants with organic fertilizer for better growth.

Pest Control

Check for pests and use natural remedies like neem oil to protect your plants.

Common Indoor Plants

Snake Plant

Aloe Vera

Easy to care for, has medicinal benefits.

Peace Lily

Thrives in shade, removes toxins from the air.

Pothos

Great for beginners, grows in low light.
